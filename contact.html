Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our adventures.

What's included in the adventure price?

Our prices include expert guides, premium equipment, accommodations, most meals, transportation during the adventure, and 24/7 support. International flights, personal gear, and some meals are typically not included.

How do you ensure safety during extreme adventures?

All our guides are certified in wilderness medicine and emergency response. We use premium equipment, maintain detailed safety protocols, provide comprehensive briefings, and have 24/7 emergency support. We also carry satellite communication devices on all remote expeditions.

What's your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation: full refund up to 60 days before departure, 50% refund up to 30 days, and credit for future adventures within 30 days. We also provide free date changes up to 30 days before departure.

Do I need previous experience for extreme adventures?

It depends on the specific adventure. Some trips are suitable for beginners with good fitness levels, while others require prior experience. We provide detailed fitness and experience requirements for each adventure and can recommend alternatives if needed.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and accessibility needs?

Absolutely! We work with you to accommodate dietary restrictions, accessibility needs, and any other special requirements. Please let us know during booking so we can make the necessary arrangements.
